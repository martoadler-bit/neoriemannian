## Frequently Asked Questions

**What tuning systems does the app support?**
Microtonal Lab supports all standard equal temperaments (12-EDO, 19-EDO, 24-EDO, 31-EDO, 41-EDO, 53-EDO, and more), as well as just intonation systems including Harry Partch's 43-tone scale.

**What is the Interval Explorer?**
A continuous slider that lets you sweep any interval from 0 to 1200 cents. It shows the nearest simple ratio, deviation from 12-EDO, and a consonance rating in real time.

**What is the Harmonic Galaxy?**
An interactive map of harmonic intervals built from pure ratios. Tap any interval to hear it, select multiple to build a scale, and save it to your library.

**How do I lock the sequencer or launchpad to a scale?**
Open the Scale Library (book icon) from any view, browse or select a scale, and tap "Use scale". The sequencer, launchpad, and keyboard all constrain to that scale's notes.

**How does the Step Sequencer work?**
Each of the 16 steps can be toggled on or off. Tap a step to set its pitch, octave, and velocity. Randomize generates patterns that stay in the active scale. Playback modes: Forward, Reverse, Ping-pong, Random.

**What is the Launchpad?**
A grid-based live performance pad. Each row is a scale degree; tap pads to trigger notes while the sequencer clock runs.

**How does MIDI export work?**
The app exports standard MIDI files from the sequencer or launchpad. Because standard MIDI only has 12 semitones, microtonal pitches are encoded using per-channel pitch bend — one channel per note — so any DAW or software synth that supports pitch bend will play back the correct microtonal tuning.

**Can I use the app with headphones?**
Yes. The app supports standard audio output including headphones, Bluetooth, and AirPlay.

**Does the app work offline?**
Yes, completely. No internet connection is required at any time.

**Which devices are supported?**
iPhone and iPad running iOS 17 or later.
