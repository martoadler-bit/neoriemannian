## Frequently Asked Questions

**What tuning systems does the app support?**
NeoRiemannian supports all standard equal temperaments (12-EDO, 19-EDO, 24-EDO, 31-EDO, 53-EDO, and more), as well as just intonation systems including Harry Partch's 43-tone scale.

**What is the Harmonic Galaxy?**
The Galaxy is an interactive map of harmonic intervals built from pure ratios. Tap any interval to hear it, select multiple intervals to build a scale, and save it to your library.

**How do I lock the sequencer or launchpad to a scale?**
Open the Scale Library (book icon) from any view, browse or select a scale, and tap "Use scale". The sequencer, launchpad, and keyboard will all constrain to that scale notes.

**How does the Step Sequencer work?**
Each of the 16 steps can be toggled on or off. Tap a step to select it, then set its pitch, octave, and velocity. Use Randomize to generate scale-aware patterns.

**What is the Launchpad view?**
A grid-based live performance view. Each row is a scale degree; tap pads to trigger notes in real time while the clock runs.

**Can I use the app with headphones?**
Yes. The app supports standard audio output including headphones, Bluetooth, and AirPlay.

**Does the app work offline?**
Yes, completely. No internet connection is required at any time.

**Which devices are supported?**
iPhone and iPad running iOS 17 or later.
