## Frequently Asked Questions

**How do I build a pitch class set?**
Open the Explorer tab and tap notes on the wheel to add or remove them from the set.

**How do I export a MIDI file?**
Go to the Sequence tab → Export, choose your source (Melody or Sequencer), and tap Export.

**What soundfonts are included?**
The app includes an upright jazz piano soundfont (UprightPianoKW) and a built-in synthesizer with multiple waveforms.

**Does the app work offline?**
Yes, completely. No internet connection is required.

**Which devices are supported?**
iPhone and iPad running iOS 17 or later.
