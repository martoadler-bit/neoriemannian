# Privacy Policy — Microtonal Lab

**Last updated: June 30, 2026**

## Overview

Microtonal Lab is a microtonal music studio for iOS. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any personal data.**

The app:
- Does not require an account or login
- Does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information
- Does not use analytics or tracking SDKs
- Does not share any data with third parties
- Does not access your contacts, location, or camera

## Audio

The app generates audio locally on your device using a built-in synthesizer. No audio is recorded or transmitted.

## Data Storage

All settings, sequences, scales, and preferences are stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device.

## Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate any third-party services, advertising networks, or analytics platforms.

## Children

The app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new effective date.
