# Privacy Policy — Neo-Riemannian

**Last updated: June 27, 2026**

## Overview

Neo-Riemannian ("the app") is an interactive tool for exploring Pitch Class Set Theory. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any personal data.**

The app:
- Does not require an account or login
- Does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information
- Does not use analytics or tracking SDKs
- Does not share any data with third parties
- Does not access your contacts, location, camera, or microphone beyond what is needed for audio playback

## Audio

The app generates audio locally on your device using a built-in synthesizer and soundfont. No audio is recorded or transmitted.

## Data Storage

All settings and preferences are stored locally on your device only, using standard iOS storage (UserDefaults). This data never leaves your device.

## Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate any third-party services, advertising networks, or analytics platforms.

## Children

The app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new date.
